## Hardware Setup
This section describes how to connect the Nordic NRF52840 Hardware Platform to EVB1000 through SPI Interface

## Interfacing EVB1000 with Nordic nRF52840
 |PIN|EVB1000|NRF52840|
 |-----|-----|-----|
 |IRQ|J6: Pin4|P0.30|
 |MISO|J6: Pin5|P0.28|
 |MOSI|J6: Pin8|P0.4|
 |SCLK|J6: Pin7|P0.3|
 |CS|J6: Pin9|P0.29|
 |GND|J6: Pin10|GND|
 |VDD|J10: Pin2|VDD|
